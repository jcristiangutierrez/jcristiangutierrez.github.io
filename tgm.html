The Godmother

In this 2D logo animation I composited the use of two softwares I consider myself very familiar with. The mediums used were Audacity, Adobe After Effects and Adobe Illustrator, to begin with I created the various vector graphics displayed in the animation using basic shapes and the pen tool. After I wrapped up in Illustrator, I exported the illustrations as a png a crucial step in order for the effects to work in After effects; following this you are to import the graphics in After effects where the magic happens. Finally in After effects I proceeded in adding the effects, in order to gain these effects you must first create a new solid with a white background, then adding the saber effect to the new solid, after that you will have to import the desired graphic and or text on to the compositon. Where you proceed to auto trace the text or vector graphic, and selecting the new masks in the properties layer then pasting them onto the saber solid. Where after to finalize and change your effect settings you will go to the effects control. In order to gain the gradual apperance of the graphics I applied a keyframe at 0% for the offest and set it back to 100% to get the full display at roughly 7 seconds. In order to achieve a more chaotic and grunge aesthetic I applied some stock footage of flames to make the video more intense. The sound I used was a grunge rock instrumental, in which I uploaded on to audacity to alter the audio. Intially the context behind this video was to create an announcement video for my clothing brand and some following merch I was going to release.
